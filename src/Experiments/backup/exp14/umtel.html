UMTEL

Usability Measurement Tool for E-Learning Sites ( UMTELS )

1. Open the e-learning web-site you want to evaluate in another browser window.
2. Select check-lists from the tabs below for evaluating features of the website.
3. Record your responses for each statement in the check-list by refering to the e-learning web-site.
4. Complete the full check-list. Do not leave any responses blank or unchecked.
5. Finally select the tab "Find Website Score" and click on "Click To Refresh Scores" to compute usability scores.
6. You can revisit any tab or check-list and change your responses. You will need to once again compute the score.
